Bernice Wahler Landscapes

Specializing in the contextual design of finely-crafted landscapes.

Bernice Wahler Landscapes specializes in the detailed design of private residences throughout New England. With over twenty years of experience, they have developed and executed landscapes that are of enduring quality and celebrate the uniqueness of their surroundings with a commitment to resilient solutions.
