Stack Brogan Collective

Building the foundation for a house of brands.

Stack Brogan Collective is a women-owned business representing a curated selection of leading furniture and textile manufacturers. At heart, they're creative professionals who happen to know the furniture design and architecture world inside and out with collectively more than 75 years of industry experience.

With a new partnership and name, Mein's objectives were to communicate the brand by creating a set of clear, meaningful, compelling messages that speak to the audiences they want to connect with and the companies that have the drive to stay at the forefront of design. Ultimately, positioning Stack Brogan to become more widely known within its key markets, be perceived as a go-to resource, and be known as a valued partner preferred by demanding customers in an industry that is constantly changing and evolving.

Branded materials focus on a rich primary color palette paired with product photography to build sophisticated and modern visuals.
